Crete is more than sun and sea it’s a land of trails, gorges and hidden gems. Around Istron you’ll find unforgettable walks for every level. Wear proper shoes, carry water, avoid midday heat and always let someone know before exploring these wild, beautiful places.

Crete’s nature invites you to explore! From gentle trails to wild gorges, Istron’s surroundings offer walks for all levels. Wear good shoes, stay hydrated and avoid hiking in peak heat.
