Step Into the Story of Lasithi
Lasithi is more than just beautiful views, it’s a place rich with history, culture and timeless charm. In this section you’ll uncover the landmarks, traditions and tales that make this place truly unique. Whether you're wandering ancient streets or visiting treasured sites, you’re walking through the soul of Lasithi. Let’s explore the past that lives on today.

Welcome to Yiamarin’s cultural and historic gems! From ancient ruins to local legends, discover the stories that shaped our town. Curious about something? Just ask we’re happy to help!

Archaeological Museum of Agios Nikolaos

Small but rich museum showcasing Minoan artifacts, local finds and the ancient history of Agios Nikolaos and its surrounding region.

Archaeological Site of Gournia

Extensive Minoan town ruins with streets, houses and a central palace. Gournia offers a vivid look into daily life in ancient Crete.

View on Maps

Ancient City of Lato

Well preserved Dorian city perched on a hilltop, featuring temples, stone streets and sweeping views of the Mirabello Bay and surrounding mountains.

Ierapetra Venetian Fortress

Coastal fortress built by the Venetians, offering a glimpse into Ierapetra’s past along with sea views and sturdy, centuries old stone walls.

Palace of Zakros

Secluded Minoan palace offering evocative ruins, sea views and insight into ancient life on Crete’s eastern edge. Peaceful, scenic and full of history.
